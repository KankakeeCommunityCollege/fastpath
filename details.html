More details

After 2 years and 60 credits in Fast Path,

you'll complete an Associate in Arts transfer degree. This degree is accepted (and guaranteed!) at 24 Illinois colleges, including all state universities. Many out-of-state colleges/universities also accept it. This means you'll start as a junior and will have completed gen-ed requirements.

The Associate in Arts is ideal for students pursuing a Bachelor of Arts, which includes areas such as communication, psychology, social work, education, history and English. If you plan to enter a more science or technology-based area, please check before enrollment to make sure this is the right program for you.

This program includes all the required courses to earn the General Education Core Curriculum stamp in Illinois.

Students who have never taken a college class or have some previous college credits are encouraged to apply.

There are some testing requirements to be ready for college. The South Extension Center advisor will review testing requirements with each applicant.

Pricing

The total cost of Fast Path for the first year is $5,562 that includes tuition, fees, and a laptop. Financial aid, scholarships and payment plans are available.

What if I don't test into English I?

Multiple measures will be used to determine your readiness for English I. Talk with your advisor about options.

What if I don't test into Statistics?

If you don't test where you need to be you can practice and retest up to five times using KCC's math testing system, ALEKS.

Can I use my own laptop?

A new laptop computer is provided and will be loaded with the tools you need.
