Fast Path - What you'll get

{% include top-banner.html %}

Convenient location

Fast Path program is offered at KCC’s Miner South Extension Center in Watseka.

A predictable schedule you can actually plan your life around

Fast Path classes will be at the same time on the same days of the week throughout the program. So whether it’s work or family obligations, or just to plan ahead, you'll have a schedule you can depend on.

A fully mapped program guaranteed to transfer

Fast Path takes the guesswork out of creating a schedule that will work. You’ll know on day one exactly what classes you’ll take, when, and where.

A rigorous curriculum designed for today’s learner

Research shows that students learn best in programs that combine both traditional and online instruction. With that in mind, Fast Path was carefully designed to maximize your engagement and success through a blend of face-to-face and online activities and materials.

A concentrated learning experience

Typically, Fast Path schedules just two classes at a time every eight weeks. This format allows you to focus on the material at a pace that maximizes learning while eliminating distractions and busy work.

A support system you can rely on

Fast Path is a cohort program, and you and your classmates will experience everything together as a team. Being part of a learning community means you’ll always have classmates to turn to for help! In addition, you will have a faculty member to be a mentor, guide, and coach throughout the program.
